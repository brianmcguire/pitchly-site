Cookies Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Cookies Policy explains how Pitchly AI uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (pitchlyai.app). It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Strictly Necessary

These cookies are required for the website to function — for example, remembering whether you have dismissed a banner or session state during checkout. They cannot be disabled in our systems.

Analytics

We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand which pages people visit and how the site performs. These cookies collect aggregate, anonymous information about visits — they do not identify you personally.

Preferences

These cookies remember choices you make (such as theme or language) so the site behaves consistently across visits.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages may embed content from third parties (for example, video providers or payment processors). Those providers may set their own cookies, governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through settings. Disabling strictly necessary cookies may make parts of the site stop working. You can usually find these controls under "Privacy" or "Security" in your browser preferences.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookies Policy as we add or change features. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.

6. Contact

Questions about cookies? Email us at hello@pitchlyai.app.
